Some of my projects can be found in my Portfolio.

This semester I am working on a platform game using Unreal Engine 4. I am the graphics programmer, developing every single material and even some of the 3D models using blender. For this role, I have experimented with procedural textures, postprocessing for outline effects and other cool effects for the graphics.

Personally, I enjoy working hard, making video games, researching and getting along with everyone.
