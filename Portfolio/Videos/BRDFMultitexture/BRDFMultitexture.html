BRDF and Multitexture (C++ and OpenGL)

This is an example that I have developed about BRDF ligthing model and multitextures in OpenGL.

The program modifies the texture coordinates of the clouds to achieve the animation.

The texture of night and day is selected and mixed according to the position of the light and the normals of the surface.
