Scara Simulator (Robot simulator: C++, Qt, SDL)

This software is a simulator for a Scara robot.

In this program, a group of 4 people worked to design and develop a simulator for the cinematic of the robot.

In this project I was in charge of the 3d graphics. Using Irrlicht (http://irrlicht.sourceforge.net/) and SDL by mean of BennuGD (http://www.bennugd.org). Also, I was in charge of some parts of the UI and the documentation (SRS and SDD documents).

Technologies:

  • BennuGD
  • Irrlicht
  • QT
  • SDL
  • C++
